BOS FACTORY PRODUCT CANVAS
VISION

A systematic approach to business observability that connects technical work to business outcomes by embedding ownership, relevance, and responsibility at every layer.

PROBLEM
  • Fragmented observability disconnected from business impact
  • No clear ownership across teams
  • 45+ minute delays in determining business impact
  • Dashboards don't evolve with business changes
  • Teams scrambling during incidents
SOLUTION

The BOS Factory:

  • Structured, repeatable process that produces business observability artifacts
  • Embeds clear ownership boundaries
  • Creates business context for all monitoring
  • Connects system alerts to business outcomes
FACTORY PROCESS
  1. DEFINE
    Business flows, steps, outcomes
  2. POPULATE
    Templates, KPIs, signals
  3. VALIDATE
    Ownership for every signal
  4. BUILD
    Dashboard requirements
  5. MONITOR
    Feedback loops, adaptation
SHARED OWNERSHIP
  • Product Teams
    Business metrics, thresholds, customer outcomes
  • Application Teams
    Business logic, process checkpoints
  • Platform Teams
    Monitoring infrastructure, dashboards

This three-way partnership is the foundation for sustainable business observability.

KEY ARTIFACTS
  • Mini-Reference Card
    • Business context and importance
    • Signals with clear ownership
    • Responsibility table for actions
  • Dashboard Panel
    • Business signals (Product-owned)
    • Process signals (App Dev-owned)
    • System signals (Platform-owned)
  • Supporting Tools
    • Persona Role Guide
    • Tactical Checklist
    • BO Template
